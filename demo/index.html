MolVision

Predicting molecular properties by showing a Vision-Language Model what a molecule looks like — not just its text formula.

The Core Idea

Why show molecules to AI?

Chemists don't just read formulas — they look at molecular diagrams to understand structure. MolVision gives AI the same ability by combining molecular images with text descriptions, using Vision-Language Models to predict properties like toxicity, solubility, and bioactivity.

Traditional approaches use text-only SMILES strings which can be ambiguous — two identical molecules can have different encodings. Visual structure captures what text misses.
